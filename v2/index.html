About

The Player

Two disciplines. One competitor.

Fedi Ben Hadj Said is a professional footballer for Étoile du Sahel in the Tunisian Ligue Professionnelle 1, representing the Tunisian national team — carrying a nation's pride onto the pitch every time he steps on it. From the academies of Tunis to the international stage, his journey is defined by discipline, resilience, and an unrelenting drive to compete at the highest level.

But his arena isn't limited to grass and stadiums. Fedi is also a professional eFootball competitor, ranking among the top players globally in Konami's eFootball championship series. The same tactical mind, split-second decision-making, and muscle memory that serve him on the pitch translate directly to the virtual pitch.

This dual career isn't a gimmick — it's a testament to a singular talent for the beautiful game, in every form it takes.
